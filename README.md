# HDT Lobby MMR — Known Streamers

This repo powers the little red streamer icon shown by the
[HDT Lobby MMR](https://github.com/zakarulcodes/HDT_LobbyMMR) plugin for
Hearthstone Deck Tracker — it shows up next to a player's name in your lobby
if they're a known Battlegrounds streamer.

The list is built from public streamer leaderboards on
[wallii.gg](https://www.wallii.gg/), plus channels streamers have linked
themselves. It updates automatically — there's nothing you need to do here.

## Are you a streamer?

If you stream Battlegrounds and want the icon to show up next to your name,
you can link your channel yourself in under a minute:

👉 **[Link your channel](https://hdt-lobbymmr-submit.zakarulcodes.workers.dev/)**

Just sign in with Twitch to confirm the channel is yours, enter your exact
in-game name, and you're done. It usually shows up within a minute or two.

Looking for the plugin itself? Head over to
[HDT_LobbyMMR](https://github.com/zakarulcodes/HDT_LobbyMMR).
